Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. VidCrunch processes all videos locally in your browser using WebAssembly. No data is ever sent to our servers.

What we don't collect:

  • Your videos or any video content
  • Personal information or accounts
  • Usage analytics or tracking
  • Cookies for data collection

Local Processing: All video compression happens entirely on your device. Your files never leave your computer.

Open Source: Our code is publicly available for review to ensure transparency.
